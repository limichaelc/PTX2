This Week's Lessons
March 30, 2014

  • FACULTY/STAFF: Your Google Docs and Google Sites can now be integrated into your Blackboard course sites for collaboration or simply sharing with your students.  Quick-start video.  Be sure to follow Princeton's Acceptable Use Policy and Usage Guidelines for Google Apps.

  • STUDENTS: Once your Princeton netID account has been closed, you will only have access to Blackboard as a "Guest," meaning you will be able to view little more than syllabi and course descriptions. Unfortunately, there is no way to mass package files from Blackboard to take with you.  It is best not to think of Blackboard as a storage space where files can be downloaded. You will have submitted files from your computer to Blackboard, so make sure that you are backing up your computer and saving your intelectual property in a place where you can reference them later.

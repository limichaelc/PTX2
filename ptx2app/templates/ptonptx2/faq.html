Frequently Asked Questions

Why is my course not showing when I search for it?

So far, we only have course data for Spring 2014 in our system. The registrar should be putting up the Fall 2014 data in September so stay tuned for that!


Why is the Amazon price from the Amazon link slightly different than the numbers on PTX 2?

Amazon updates their numbers up to several times a day. We retrieve the price information less often than that. However, the prices tend to remain pretty much the same.
